Volunteers Needed

Come join us in making this tournament a successful fundraiser for the Royal Ottawa Foundation for Mental Health, Youville Centre, the Shepherds of Good Hope - Hope Living Facility and Knights of Columbus Charities - Coats for Kids Program and Food for Families Program.

Why

Volunteers are need to assist at our golf registration table, selling Raffle Board tickets and running our Holes-in-One and Skills Contest holes.

Our volunteers also help run the draw for the Raffle Board.

When and Where

Volunteers should arrive by 11:00 a.m. at the Loch March Golf & Country Club, 1755 Old Carp Rd.

More information on what to wear and the schedule for the day will be provided to all volunteers before the day of the tournament, Tuesday, May 23rd, 2023.
