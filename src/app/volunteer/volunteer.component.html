Volunteers Needed

The Knights of Columbus needs your help to make our 2024 Charity Golf Tournament a successful fundraiser for our Charities, and an event to remember for all the participants. Thank you in advance, your assistance is vital to our success.

What can I do?

We are seeking volunteers to help on the day of the event (Tuesday, May 21, 2024) with a variety of tasks, including but not limited to:

  • golfer registration;
  • selling Raffle Board tickets;
  • running the Holes-in-One and Skills Contests holes;
  • handling the Raffle Board draw; and
  • the silent auction.

When and where do I need to be?

Volunteers should arrive by 11:00 a.m. on Tuesday, March 21st at the Loch March Golf & Country Club (1755 Old Carp Rd, Kanata, ON K2K 1X7).

More information on what to wear and the schedule for the day will be provided to all volunteers via email before the tournament.
