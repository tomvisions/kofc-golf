Calling all golfers!

May 2024 is quickly approaching and the Knights of Columbus Council 9544 is looking forward to hosting our Annual Charity Golf Tournament at the Loch March Golf & Country Club in Kanata.

Please complete the registration form below and join us on May 21, 2024 for a wonderful day of golf, followed by a buffet supper with a Silent Auction. There will also be a Raffle Board and lots of prizes to be won.

Registration costs $175/person (maximum 4 players per team), which includes green fees, a golf cart and the buffet supper. Please note that individual golfers will be placed into teams on the day of the event.

Thank you in advance for your participation and for helping raise funds for our charities.This year proceeds will support three local charities:

  • The Youville Centre
  • The Catholic Centre for Immigrants
  • Shepherds of Good Hope - Hope Living Centre in Kanata

To register, please complete the form below. Register by May 2nd.
