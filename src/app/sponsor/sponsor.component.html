Sponsorship

Please help make the 2024 Knights of Columbus Golf Tournament a success by becoming a Corporate Sponsor, and supporting our Charities that provide vital services to the vulnerable, disadvantaged and needy in our community. For those who are unable to provide financial support, we also welcome in-kind donations of goods and/or services that will be used as prizes throughout the event, including for the Raffle Board and Silent Auction. All funds raised through the Raffle Board and Silent Auction will be donated to our charities.

Benefits of becoming a Corporate Sponsor

All Corporate Sponsors will enjoy:

  • Visibility of company name by at least 120 golfers on the day of the event
  • Visibility of company name and logo on Annual Charity Golf Tournament website
